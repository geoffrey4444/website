Workshop on Gravitational Waves and High Performance Computing 2024

California State University, Fullerton

Students from Citrus College will participate in the 2024 Cal State Fullerton Workshop on High-Performance Computing and Gravitational Waves.

The workshop will be held at Cal State Fullerton, hosted by Nicholas and Lee Begovich Center for Gravitational-Wave Physics and Astronomy, on August 12, 2024 – August 16, 2024, in McCarthy Hall room 629 (campus map).

Applying

To apply for the 2024 workshop, please contact Marianne Smith. If you have questions about the workshop, please don’t hesitate to contact me.
